Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Expense Tracker – Budget App developed by HILAL NORD MA.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information
We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, phone number, bank details, or payment information.

b) Financial Data Entered by the User
Any expense data you enter (titles, amounts, categories, dates) is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload your data to external servers.

c) Device / Technical Information
The app may process limited technical information (such as app version, device model, and Android version) only for basic compatibility and stability purposes. If crash reporting or analytics are added in future updates, we will update this policy accordingly.

2. Data Storage & Security

Your data remains on your device. We implement reasonable safeguards, but no electronic storage method is 100% secure.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal or financial data with third parties.

4. Third‑Party Services

At this time, the app does not use third‑party services that collect user data. If we add services such as analytics, advertising, or cloud backup in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide details.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Control

Because data is stored locally, you control your information. You can delete your data anytime by clearing the app’s data or uninstalling the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
